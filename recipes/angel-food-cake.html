Angel Food Cake

Decription

This classic dessert is light bodied, not too rich, and goes great with strawberries and whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cake flour
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar, divided
  • 12 egg whites
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
