Chicken Soup

Description

This simple, classic, and hearty dish is a staple of home cooking, often made when feeling ill, but just as enjoyable even at the pinnacle of good health.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 4 carrots halved
  • 4 celery stalks halved
  • 1 large onion halved
  • water to cover
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon chicken bullion
