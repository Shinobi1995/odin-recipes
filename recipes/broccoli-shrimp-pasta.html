Broccoli Shrimp Pasta

Description

This is a relatively quickly prepared dish that uses common ingredients, amd does not sacrifce deliciousness for ease of preperation. Larger fresh shrimp are ideal, but it can made more inexpensively by using small frozen cocktail shrimp, or my substituting a different protein (or be made vegetarian by skipping the protein completely, as the lemony-garlicky taste will still be quite good)

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces whole wheat penne (cooked according to package instructions)
  • One quarter cup extra virgin olive oil (divided)
  • 1 pound raw shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • One half teaspoon pepper
  • 4 cloves garlic (grated)
  • 2 large heads broccoli, cut into florets (about 4 cups)
  • 1 juice and zest of lemon
  • parmesan cheese
  • Crushed red pepper flakes to serve (optional if more heat is desired)
